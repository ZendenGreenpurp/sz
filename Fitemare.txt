Fitemare concept
----------------
Scruffy waster Colin Dennis falls asleep drunk in a portaloo in a building site.
Whilst sleeping he is possesed by ancient spirits that were disturbed by the building site on their burial ground.
He is possessed and while he sleeps is forced to confront his demons in a msytic dream labyrith called the Fitemare.
